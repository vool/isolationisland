Isolation Island

Due to the COVID-19 pandemenic the Irish govenment introduced restrictions on unnessary travel which meant that you could only exercise within 2 kilometres of your home.
This website will generate you own personal 2 kilometre Isolation Island and with the help of Open Data will help you discover some interesting locations within these limits.

Created by Keith Phelan with data from National Monuments Service and National Inventory of Architectural Heritage
